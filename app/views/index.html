Digital Apprenticeship Service

The way the government funds apprenticeships in England is changing in early 2017. Some employers will be required to contribute to a new apprenticeship levy, and there will be changes to the funding for apprenticeship training for all employers, whether you pay the levy or not.

You only pay the levy if your payroll bill is in excess of £3million per year.

If you’re not a levy payer

If you don’t pay the apprenticeship levy you can still use the service to recruit an apprentice or post vacancies.

You can:

  • select an apprenticeship framework or standard
  • choose the training provider or providers you want to deliver the training
  • choose an assessment organisation
  • post apprenticeship vacancies
